My Projects

Bitchin' Tweets
April 27, 2016

For my Big-Data class at the University of Colorado Boulder, we created this visual display to show the overall sentiment in each of the fifty States. This is a heat-map representation of the "Bitchyness-level" of tweets from across the nation. Many people use Twitter to express themselves, and we wanted to see if there would be noticeable differences in the negativity or positivity of tweets. This was a semester long project, and my respoinsiblilty was the design and execution of the website. If you want to know more about this project, checkout the website!

Chip Tracker!
February 19, 2016

This was a project that lasted two weeks in my User Centered Design and Developement class. This class is mainly focused on web developement! Our assignment this week was to make an app that used people (clients) and service providers (admins) location data, similiar to what Uber does. We made an app that could help Chip the Buffalo (CU Boulder's Mascot) send out his location to all the fans.
